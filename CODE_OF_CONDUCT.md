# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and community members, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Respectful communication** — Using welcoming and inclusive language
- **Active listening** — Considering diverse viewpoints and experiences
- **Collaborative problem-solving** — Working together to resolve issues
- **Professional conduct** — Focusing on the code, not the person
- **Inclusive participation** — Welcoming contributions from all backgrounds

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary language based on any protected characteristic
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, spam, or disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any form of intimidation or threatening behavior

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. Violations may result in:

1. **Warning** — For first minor violations
2. **Temporary Suspension** — For repeated or serious violations
3. **Permanent Ban** — For severe or persistent harassment

## Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Report privately** — Email: founder@nbr.company
2. **Provide details** — Include who, what, when, where, and context
3. **Request confidentiality** — If needed, we will respect your privacy
4. **Follow-up** — We'll investigate and respond within 72 hours

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All GitHub repositories and discussions
- Project-related events and forums
- Official communication channels
- Public spaces where community members identify with the project

## Contact

- **Questions:** founder@nbr.company
