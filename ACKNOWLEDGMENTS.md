## Inspirations & References

### Design Patterns & Principles
- **Resilience Patterns:** Building on decades of distributed systems research
- **Circuit Breaker Pattern:** Inspired by Michael T. Nygard's "Release It!"
- **Health-Driven Selection:** Influenced by modern load balancing and observability
- **Clean Architecture:** Following Robert C. Martin's principles

### Open Source Communities
- **Python Community:** For an excellent ecosystem and standards
- **Request Library:** HTTP abstraction patterns
- **Pytest:** Gold standard in Python testing
- **GitHub:** For hosting and collaboration platform

### Academic & Industry Research
- **Chaos Engineering:** Netflix, Gremlin, and chaos community
- **Distributed Systems:** Consensus algorithms and failure modes
- **Observability:** Charity Majors and industry best practices
- **Security:** OWASP and security community standards
